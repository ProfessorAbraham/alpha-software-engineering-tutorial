Frequently Asked Questions
Quick answers to common questions about ASET programs

What are the prerequisites for the programs?

No prior programming experience is required. Our programs are designed for beginners to advanced learners.

How long do I have access to the course materials?

You get lifetime access to all course materials, including updates and new content.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with the program.

Can I switch between packages after enrollment?

Yes, you can upgrade your package at any time by paying the difference.
